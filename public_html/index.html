SnapPark

Find a spot in a snap

How do I use this App?

Your current location is shown with an arrow in the middle of the map. By default, the nearest available parking spots will be shown.
  • To get details about a spot, click the icon.
  • To reserve a spot, click it, select an amount of time you want it for and click "Reserve This Spot".
  • To recenter the map, click the map or drag the location arrow.
  • Change what is displayed by adjusting the controls in the upper right corner.
